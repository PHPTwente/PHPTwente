PHPTwente Meetup
6 September - Enschede

Thursday 6 september there will be a PHPTwente meetup in Enschede!

The topic will be how we can do more fun and interesting things for and with PHP developers in Twente?
(And, on a side note, who will be going to the PFCongres on 15 september?)

Would you like to join the conversation, have you got ideas you'd like to share or would you simply like to show an interest? Join us!

If you decide to come, please let us know at on our meetup page so we can get a rough idea of how many people will show up.

The meeting starts at 19:00 and will be held at "de Bakspieker" (de brasserie of hotel "De Broeierd"), Hengelosestraat 725, Enschede.
