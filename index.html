PHPTwente Meetup
4 October - Enschede

Thursday 4 October we are having our second official PHPTwente meetup!

Would you like to talk to other PHP developers, have some fun or drink a beer or two? Join us!

If you decide to come, please let us know at on our meetup page so we can get a rough idea of how many people will show up.

The meetup starts at 19:00 and will be held at the offices of TriMM located at Moutlaan 25 in Enschede.
